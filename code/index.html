A Harpejji Story:

The Discovery that Transformed Stevie Wonder's Music

Hi!

I'm Joyce Kuo.

I'm a guitarist, web developer, and a passionate advocate for spreading awareness about the harpejji, an extraordinary and versatile electric stringed instrument.

I'm not a representative of the company who manufactures the instrument. I'm just a musician who had the incredible opportunity to share it with a legend. Here's how it all happened, and how the harpejji could inspire you too.

A New Instrument with Untapped Potential

It was winter 2012, and I was just a local musician who was fortunate enough to attend the National Association of Music Merchants (NAMM), an invitation-only trade show where musicians and industry insiders gather to experience the latest in musical innovation.

As I wandered through the bustling aisles, I stumbled across an instrument I had never seen before: the harpejji. Its design immediately caught my eye, so I decided to give it a try. I was drawn to the instrument's unique blend of tactile interaction and beautiful sound and I had a feeling that it had real potential to break new ground in music.

An Unexpected Encounter with Stevie Wonder

Later that day, I found myself chatting with someone connected to Stevie Wonder's inner circle. Before long, I was within earshot of the legend himself as he played a hammer dulcimer and expressed his desire to feel more connected to the strings. It was in that moment that I felt compelled to speak up and ask if he had ever played the harpejji. He hadn't heard of it, but Stevie was intrigued and insisted I take him to this new instrument so he could try it out for himself.

Stevie's Connection to the Harpejji

Watching Stevie Wonder play the harpejji for the first time was a surreal experience. As soon as his fingers touched the strings, it was clear that the instrument spoke to him in a way that few others had. It offered the direct, tactile connection he had been seeking, and his excitement was infectious.

Soon after, Stevie had a custom harpejji made for him, and the instrument became a major part of his songwriting, recording, and performing.

The Power of Spreading Good Information

The story of the harpejji and Stevie Wonder is testament to the power of sharing knowledge. Sometimes all it takes is connecting the right person with the right tool to create something extraordinary. Watching an artist like Stevie incorporate the harpejji into his work showed me how powerful a single idea, or a single introduction, can be.

An Instrument Worthy of More Attention

One challenge with the harpejji is that it is made to order and not something you can easily find in stores, so it remains somewhat under the radar. That's why I've made it my mission to help more people discover this remarkable instrument.

It's not just about shining a spotlight on the harpejji; it's about ensuring that everyone - especially those who may not have access to traditional instruments - has the opportunity to connect with their music in new, innovative ways.

Making a Tiny Ripple in the Ocean of Music

Looking back on that day at NAMM and everything that followed, I'm proud to have played a small role in the story of the harpejji, and I'm excited to see where it goes next. The instrument has already changed music for one legend, and I believe it can do the same for many others.

Join the Journey

If you're curious about the harpejji, I invite you to learn more. Whether you're a musician looking for a new way to express yourself, or just someone fascinated by innovative instruments, the harpejji is worth your attention. After linking Stevie with Marcodi and the creator of the harpejji, I have coordinated other demos, and I'm always happy to connect with people eager to experience this instrument.
