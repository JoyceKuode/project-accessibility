How I Influenced Stevie Wonder's Music Career: A True Story About Impacting a Legend

Hi!

My name is Joyce Kuo, and this is a story about the time I met Stevie Wonder - an encounter that would influence his music in a way I could never have predicted.

It was winter 2012, and I was a humble local musician who was fortuante enough to attend NAMM, an invitation-only trade show where musicians and industry insiders gather to experience the latest in musical innovation.

A Serendipitous Discovery

As I walked through the bustling halls of NAMM, surrounded by all the latest and greatest in music technology, I stumbled across an instrument I had never seen before. As a musician with experience in both guitar and piano, I was immediately captivated by its design, which seemed to blend elements of both instruments. Naturally, I decided to give it a try.
