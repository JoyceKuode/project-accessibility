A Harpejji Story:

The Discovery that Transformed Stevie Wonder's Music

A New Instrument with Untapped Potential

It was winter 2012, and I was a local musician fortunate enough to attend the National Association of Music Merchants(NAMM), an invitation-only trade show where musicians and industry insiders gather to experience the latest in musical innovation.

As I wandered through the bustling aisles, filled with sounds of new and exciting instruments, I stumbled across a new instrument I had never seen before: the harpejji. At first glance, it looked like a cross between a piano and a guitar. Its design immediately intrigued me, so I decided to give it a try. I was drawn to the instrument's unique blend of tactile interaction and beautiful sound. I had a feeling that this was more than just another instrument, but it had real potential to break new ground in music
